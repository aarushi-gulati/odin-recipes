Lasagna

Description

For this recipe, we are essentially making a thick, meaty tomato sauce and layering that with noodles and cheese into a casserole.

Steps:

  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
